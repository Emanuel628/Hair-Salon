# Copy Direction

## Brand Voice

The copy should sound:

- Warm
- Confident
- Human
- Premium without being cold
- Clear without sounding robotic
- Personal without sounding cheesy

Avoid generic salon language like:

- "Book your appointment today"
- "We offer the best services"
- "Look and feel your best"
- "Pamper yourself"

The copy should feel like a stylist who understands their client and knows how to guide them.

## Core Message

The salon website should communicate:

> Your hair should feel like you.

The stylist is not just performing a service. They are helping the client feel confident, polished, and understood.

## Copy Rules

- Keep paragraphs short
- Make every section guide the user somewhere
- Do not overload the homepage
- Avoid generic filler
- Use emotional language carefully
- Keep booking actions obvious
- Make the stylist feel personal and trustworthy
